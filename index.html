About Me

Hello! My name is Fernando, I'm 18 years old and I'm an amateur music producer. My favourite genres are melodic dubstep, drum and bass, artcore, dariacore, hyperpop and much more. I currently play cello at Esproarte (Escola Profissional de Artes de Mirandela) and have been playing since almost 3 years ago when I started studying there. I publish my projects under the alias "strog" or "notstrog" and here I archive most of them. In case you want to message me, you can do so via the Instagram or Email that are located at the bottom of the page.
